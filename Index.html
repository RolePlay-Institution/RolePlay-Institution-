Battle RolePlay

Battle Roleplay, originally Roleplay Fighting. It's a hectic thing to do that sucks in every single cell that you need running at the start of the day. It involves knowledge, wit, strategy, and a mind working at full throttle. People even put their accounts and pride on the line! The thing is, what's so addictive? I'd consider it as the thrill of imagination being put to words. Forging stories, creating this and that. The only downside is when you've known too much in comparison to the people around you, wanting to ascend yet your resources prove futile. Battle Roleplay isn't about the portrayal of characters, may they be original or canon, but the way someone paints what is in mind on a canvas where words intertwine, creating an interactive story. Many people forgot one of the key points of Text-based roleplay, it's enjoyment and improvement without involving deep-end pride and ego. People forgot that roleplay also embodies a way to tell an own-made story.

Basic RolePlay Rules

➣ No Coding

This Concept Means To The Use Of Intense Synonyms To Hide Your Initial Actions, Though Other Battle Roleplay Styles Such As Aesthetic,KJV,Wordic,Biblic,etc... Are Allowed.

➣ No Time Manipulation

This Connotation Refers To The Interference In The Rate Of Which Continuity Is Progressing Throughout Space, This Is Action Is Not Allowed No Matter What Scale.

➣ No Meta-Gaming

Meta-gaming Refers To Using INFORMATION That A CC Doesn't Know Just Because You Figured It Because He's Done A Certain Action Or Has A Certain Rank Doesn't Mean He Knows That Skill Unless Proven So In The Canon Series Or Manga.

➣ No Power-Playing

Powerplaying Refers To Using ABILITIES That A CC Has Never Used Before Just Because You Figured It Because He's Done A Certain Action Or Has A Certain Rank Doesn't Mean He Knows That Skill Unless Proven So In The Canon Series Or Manga.

➣ No God-Modding

God-Modding Refers To Being Completely Invincible Throughout The Entire Battle And Having No Known Weakness On Whatsoever.

➣ No Puppeteering

Puppeteering Refers To Stating Your Opponent's Actions In Your Script That Your Opponent Has Never Stating Engaging In In Order To Fit Your Personal Advantage.

➣ No Regeneration | Healing

Regeneration Refers To Repairing Your Body Instantly After An Attack Has Affected Your Body Negatively.

➣ No Auto Attacks

This Refers To Stating Offensive Actions And Dictating Their Successfull Effect Without Allowing Your Opponent To Counter. Rather State "In An Attempt To..." Before Stating The Effect Of The Offensive Action Used.

➣ No Auto Defences

This Refers To Stating You Were Successfully Able To Block Or Counter Without A Realistic And Vivid Explanation.

➣ No Backtracking

The Concept Of Backtracking Is When You Attempt To Make An Attack On Your Turn That Realistically Would Have Affected Your Opponent’s Attack On His Last Turn.

➣ No Space-Time Manipulation

This Actions Refers To Use Of Techniques Such As Warping, Teleporting Or Wormholes.
